Creating a new Trigger
You can modify a Trigger once it was created. You can change the type of the Trigger as well as modify its components. To change the type of a Trigger,
  • Open the Trigger Preferences
  • Highlight the Trigger you want to modify
  • Choose the new type by pressing the type icon in the list entry
To modify the components of a Trigger, e.g. the Delay of a Mouse Trigger, or the Key-Combination of a Keyboard Trigger,
  • Open the Trigger Preferences
  • Highlight the Trigger you want to modify
  • Press the Info button to bring up the Information panel
